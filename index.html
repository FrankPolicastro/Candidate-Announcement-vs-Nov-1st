The Shape of Recent US Presidential Elections

Can the makeup of past US Presidential Election candidates be a predictor of future elections? This visualization draws upon
Candidate's party affiliation, candidacy 'announced' and 'withdrawal' dates, and the length of candidates' election campaigns to
see if any insights can be derived.
